Adoptify is more than a platform—it's a mission to ensure every displaced animal finds safety and a loving home.

Our Vision

Founded in 2024, Adoptify serves as the bridge between rescue organizations, individual pet owners, and animal lovers. We leverage technology to reduce the time animals spend in shelters, provide instant rescue reporting for emergencies, and verify NGO partners to ensure the highest standards of care.

Frequently Asked Questions

Browse our "Find Pets" section, select an animal you love, and click "Adopt Me". You'll need to fill out a short application. Once the owner reviews and approves it, you can coordinate the meeting!

Most adoptions on Adoptify are free, as we focus on rescues. However, some NGOs may ask for a small donation to cover medical costs. This will be clearly stated in the pet's profile.

Log in to your account, go to "Add Animal", and provide details and photos. Your listing will be instantly visible to thousands of potential adopters.

Your report is broadcasted to all verified NGOs nearby. You will receive notifications as soon as an NGO accepts the mission and begins their journey to the location.
