Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Last Updated: April 12, 2024

1. User Privacy

At Adoptify, your privacy is paramount. We collect personal data such as your name, email, and location solely to facilitate the adoption and rescue process. We do not sell your data to third parties. Your location is shared with NGOs only when you report a rescue emergency.

2. Adoption Responsibility

Adoptify provides a platform for connecting pets with owners. We are not responsible for the direct care, behavior, or medical history of animals listed. Users are encouraged to conduct their own due diligence and meet in public places for the initial animal handover.

3. NGO Verification

Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) must provide valid license numbers and organization details to access the rescue dashboard. While we verify these details with administrative staff, we do not guarantee the actions of individual volunteer organizations.

4. Prohibited Content

Selling animals for profit is strictly prohibited on Adoptify. Any user found listing animals for commercial breeding or sales purposes will have their account permanently suspended without notice.
